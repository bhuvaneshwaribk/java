Preposition usage for Vehicles:

If you can stand and walk inside a vehicle, then use 'on'.

ex: I've travelled London on boat.

If not, use 'in'.

ex: I come to office in car.

Note: For bicycle/bikes, use 'on'

ex: I like to travel on bike.

Also, in general, can use by

ex: He came by car
